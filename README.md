# DreamWeave

**Sleep data is often scattered across devices and still difficult to read as a clear night story. DreamWeave brings together sleep-related data across connected devices to help users understand each night more clearly through a calmer, more readable view of sleep.**

## Why DreamWeave

Sleep data can be rich and useful, but it is often fragmented across devices and harder to interpret than it should be.

For many users, understanding a single night of sleep still means moving between different tools, different graphs, and different assumptions. CPAP data may live in one place, oxygen and pulse data in another, and broader health context somewhere else. Even when the data is available, turning it into a clear picture of what happened during the night can take more effort than it should.

DreamWeave starts from a simple goal: make each night easier to read, easier to understand, and easier to revisit.

It is designed for people who want more than raw graphs, but less than overconfident medical conclusions. DreamWeave aims to bring scattered sleep-related data into a clearer and more readable night-by-night view.

## Built around connected sleep data

DreamWeave is built around the idea that sleep should be understood across signals, devices, and time.

Rather than treating each device as a separate destination, DreamWeave brings related sleep data together into a shared timeline and a more unified report. This can include CPAP data, oxygen and pulse data, wearable signals, and broader health context when available.

The goal is not to replace clinical judgment or make diagnostic claims. The goal is to help users see patterns more clearly, reduce interpretation burden, and better understand what happened during the night.

## What DreamWeave offers

### Bring sleep-related data together

DreamWeave gathers connected sleep-related data into one place so users no longer have to jump between separate tools just to understand a single night.

### Make each night easier to read

DreamWeave is designed to turn complex sleep data into a calmer and more readable experience. Instead of starting with dense graphs alone, it aims to help users quickly understand the most important parts of the night.

### Connect patterns to the timeline

Key issues and notable patterns can be tied back to specific moments in the night. This makes it easier to move from summary to evidence, and from evidence back to understanding.

### Keep important metrics within reach

Night-level metrics such as therapy usage, event counts, leak-related indicators, pressure-related values, oxygen stability, and other relevant signals should remain visible without overwhelming the interface.

### Support deeper exploration when needed

DreamWeave is designed to feel approachable first, while still allowing users to dig deeper when they want more detail. The goal is not to hide the data, but to make depth feel navigable.

## Designed to reduce interpretation burden

DreamWeave is meant to feel less like a wall of data and more like a readable night report.

On a good night, it should help users confirm that things look stable and understandable.

On a difficult night, it should help users see what stood out, where it happened, and which signals are worth looking at first.

The product value is not just data access. It is the ability to return to a night, understand it more calmly, and feel less lost in the process.

## Early direction

DreamWeave begins with a simpler promise: make connected sleep data easier to read.

Over time, it may grow into a richer sleep insights product built on stronger data integration, clearer night reports, and more thoughtful interpretation. But the foundation remains the same: help people understand each night more clearly.
